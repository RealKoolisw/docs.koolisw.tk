# How to use RoVer?

First. you are needed to type **!verify** command in to verify channel, and wait until the bot provides you the website URL\(Website is trustable and safe\), click the link.

![](../.gitbook/assets/img_6280.png)

Click the link, and click **sign in with discord**, login with your **Discord** Account!

![](../.gitbook/assets/img_6281.png)

After you finished login, it will show the page like this, type your ROLBOX Username in the blank yellow lines circled, and click **adding a code to your profile**

![](../.gitbook/assets/img_6282.jpeg)

Next, it will show a lot of words. just needed to click **copy to clipboard** to copy all the words. 

![](../.gitbook/assets/img_6283.png)

Go to your ROBLOX profile page, type words copied, and save it.

![](../.gitbook/assets/img_6285.png)

Back to the website, and click **done**

![](../.gitbook/assets/img_6286.png)

Done Verification

![](../.gitbook/assets/img_6287.png)

**Still not ended!** back to the verify channel, type command **!verify**

![](../.gitbook/assets/img_6288.png)

Thank you! You have already done verification!

![](../.gitbook/assets/img_6289.png)
